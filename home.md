## Sections (top → bottom)

1. **Hero** + the two CTAs above.

2. **What's new — two tabs: `Recently added` · `Trending`.**
   Both, not either/or. Early on *Recently added* (recency) carries the section;
   *Trending* ranks by the auto-fetched `github_stars` / activity signal. This is
   where a fresh community port surfaces (e.g. "flock-zorch just landed").

3. **Leaderboard teasers** — three compact top-3 cards: **PCS · Hash · zkVM**,
   each linking to the full board. Shows the site has *живой* comparative data,
   not just a list.

4. **Contribute band** — one line on the PR-only data flow + a link to the
   Contribute page. This is the growth engine; keep it visible on the home page.

5. **Footer** — repeat nav, links to the data repo, schema, and license.
